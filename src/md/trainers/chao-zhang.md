Chao Zhang is a PhD candidate at Wageningen University & Research (WUR), working with both the Artificial Intelligence Group and the Agriculture Biosystems Engineering Group. He earned his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Agricultural Engineering from Jiangsu University and Zhejiang University, China. His research focuses on bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI and agriculture. Chao applies advanced methodologies such as Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval, and Time Series Forecasting to agricultural contexts. Through interdisciplinary collaborations, he aims to solve complex challenges in modern farming systems.
