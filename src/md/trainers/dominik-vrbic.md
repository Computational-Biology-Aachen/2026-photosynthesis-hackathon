## About Dominik

Dominik is Program Manager at the Jan Ingenhousz Institute in Wageningen, The Netherlands, where he leads the development of OpenJII, an open-source agricultural IoT platform for scientific research. With a background in computer science and business, Dominik architects the platform's data systems, cloud infrastructure, and sensor integrations, with a focus on FAIR data principles and open science standards. He works closely with research partners across institutions to make high-quality sensor data more accessible and actionable for the global research community.
