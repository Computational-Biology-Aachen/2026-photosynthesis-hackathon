# Summary

Ifeoluwa Simeon Odesina is a Doctoral student in the Genetics and Plant Breeding program, Universidade Estadual do Norte Fluminense Darcy Ribeiro (UENF), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. He is currently a CAPES scholar and a recipient of the Brazilian mobility scholarship under the auspice of Prof. Rosana Rodrigues, the team lead for the breeding for disease resistance program in pepper and bean, UENF. His research interests center on precision breeding and the integration of phenomics in legume improvement. He envisions contributing to teams developing real-time models of radiation use efficiency and exploring its nexus with crop improvement which resonates with the objectives of the Photosynthesis hackathon events coming up in Ghana. He looks forward to meeting and learning from experienced trainers and co-participants. He is very optimistic that it will be a great event.
