# Summary

Olalekan Obisesan is a Professor of Statistics trained at both the University of Ibadan (UI) and the University College London (UCL). As an academic staff at UI he rose through the ranks from Assistant Lectureship to the rank of Reader (Associate Professorship) after which he was appointed Professor of Statistics by the Governing Council of the First Technical University Ibadan in April 2022. His area of interests includes Statistical Modelling of Environmental and Agricultural Data Sets, Biometrics Methods and Computational Methods. He has taught his Statistical Modelling courses in several institutions including the University of Ibadan, Ladoke Akintola University of Technology Ogbomoso, University College London, The University of Melbourne Australia, AIMS South Africa and First Technical University Ibadan. He has consulted and still consulting as a Statistical Consultant to the World Bank, FMoH, Datametrics and so on.

He is extremely motivated in participating in this HACKATHON so as to consolidate his statistical modelling background and to train others on his exposure to photosynthesis. He seeks to be able to understand data so as to explore their hidden details and allow the data to speak for themselves for sustainable development.
