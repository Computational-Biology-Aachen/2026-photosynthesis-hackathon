# Summary

Wajiha Mu’az Abdullahi Ph.D is a Lecturer/Researcher with Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. Has been a fellow of International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) Kano Station since her SIWES where she was exposed to real context of science. She is a resource person in the
Plant Breeding community on driving United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UNSDGs) and has also been involved in both National and International research projects. She is an advocate of gardening where every home will be a safe place for food and nutritional security
thereby by increasing the income of the household. She has acquired relevant training and experience in managing and implementing agricultural research works in the course of her career. 

She is proficient in the use of key computer applications and several data analysis software. She has good communication and presentation skills including the talent to develop good reports, concept notes and content for publications. She has conducted several training skills through
mentoring orientation workshops, leadership skills, gender- responsive research partnership and networks that has sharpen scientific skills of women and youth.
